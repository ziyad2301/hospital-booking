About Us

Metro Hospital

The hospital had a very humble beginning in 1990. Krishnarajapuram was a tiny village with barely any facility to address the health needs, of the local community. It was with this in mind that the People of Bengaluru started this hospital.
It is also a recognized research centre. The hospital is conveniently located with easy access by road, rail and air. Bengaluru Railway Station is only 2 kms away. Krishnarajapuram is the meeting point of National Highway 47 and the Main Central Road.

OUR VISION
“A journey toward excellence providing comprehensive health care with compassion”

OUR MISSION
“Develop a health care service at an affordable cost and facilitate professional excellence to the health care providers to achieve the integral health vision of the nation”
